Narator

The girl sighed as she closed her notebook, feeling a bit frustrated with the lack of inspiration. Her balcony offered a peaceful view of the sky, but it seemed that the creative spark she needed wasn't present in the moment. As she stood up, her stomach growled loudly, a reminder that she had neglected her own needs.

Deciding to take a break from her writing, she headed to the kitchen only to find nothing. With a resigned look, she realized it was time to go out for some groceries. Grabbing her keys and slipping on a light jacket, she left her apartment in search of nourishment.
